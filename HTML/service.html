Birth Certificate Issuance

The service enables parents to register and activate the birth event electronically, and to request delivery of documents via Post, without the need to visit civil status offices.

Start service

Service steps

  1. Log in to Absher Portal.
  2. Choose birth registration service.
  3. Enter required personal information.
  4. Record birth registration.
  5. Request documents and delivery.

Prerequisites

  • The existence of a registered and activated Absher account for the beneficiary.
  • The existence of a registered national address of the beneficiary.
  • The existence of an electronic communication received from the Ministry of Health.
  • More details in: the Manual for using Civil Affairs services.
